Analog Face for Metawatch
-------------------------

Standalone APK which synchronizes with
- Metawatch Community Edition
- Metawatch Noah Edition

Installation:
- Launch MetawatchAnalogFace.apk on your mobile
- Launch Metawatch Analog Face app
- Open Metwatach NE or CE, wait for the application to appear in "Digital Watch App" and select it.
- you now can select your options:
	- through the Metawatch Analog Face application: play with the options and check the watch display
	- through the watch buttons: (1) cycles through no date, date only, date + day of the week
				     (2) toggles date position, 9 and 3, or 6
				     (3) toggles frame display around date fields
